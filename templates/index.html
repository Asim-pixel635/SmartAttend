How to Use

  1. Register Students: Enter a name and click "Register Student". The webcam will open to capture the student's face.
  2. Mark Attendance: Click "Mark Attendance" and look at the webcam. The system will automatically recognize the student and mark attendance.
  3. View Records: Use the refresh buttons to see who has marked attendance.
  4. Duplicate Prevention: A student can only mark attendance once per day.
